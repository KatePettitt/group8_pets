Benny's Fact File

Introduction

Benny is my family's dog, although he was technically a birthday gift for my sister. He's a classic mutt, and we adopted him from a pig farm in central California. He is quite quirky as a result of being a farm dog who now lives in Los Angeles, but we love him regardless!

Behavior

Benny is a very active dog, he loves barking, digging, playing fetch, and going on runs with my mom along the beach. He loves to dig holes in the sand and spray sand on anyone who stands too close behind him.

Diet

Benny has a very sensitive stomach for a farm dog, so he can only eat his kibble. He loves the occasional pup cup from Starbucks as a treat though!

Fun Fact

Benny loves to play hide and seek with you and will patiently wait while you hide!
