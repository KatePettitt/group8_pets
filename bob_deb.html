Bob and Deb's Fact File

Introduction

We rehomed Bob and Deb during the COVID 2020 pandemic. When we adopted them they were called 'Rita and Blondie' but we preferred Bob and Deb.

Behavior

Guinea pigs are such friendly creatures and make the best house pets. They love food and let you know when it's breakfast snack and dinner time and love to snuggle on the sofa with you. Especially if you have a fleece blanket they can curl up in.

Diet

As much food as they can stomach! Salad and vegetables as well as dry food such as hay and guinea pig biscuits. But a run around in the grass with a few dandelions will get them weeeeeking the loudest!

Fun Fact

- They were very particular about their veg and would turn their noses up at anything that was slightly past its best.
