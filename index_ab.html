Nikkus Fact File

Introduction

I got Nikku for my 16th birthday. My family were always against getting a pet until suddenly my mum brought him on. After that day, he became part of the family and he always will be.

Behavior

My cat uses me for his food and cuddles. He also enjoys hunting wrapping papers but have slowly started moving onto mice (which is not fun because I am deathly afraid of them).

Diet

My cat will eat anything under the sun. Just the other day I caught him licking charcoal (hes okay). Although he absolutely hates turkey, which I am not sure why.

Fun Fact

- He absolutely hates catnip. No matter how many times I've tried to give it to him he runs away like hes scared of it.
